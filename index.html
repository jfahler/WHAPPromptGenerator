AP World History Random SAQ Generator

About this tool:

This tool is designed to help teachers and students alike with creating practice questions to help develop skills and practice for the AP exam. It was designed by an AP World History teacher with the intent of creating a more dynamic and somewhat unpredictable test practice activity.

Please note that this tool is in its early stages and because of the random nature and large scope, may produce weird results.

It was heavily inspired by the Educational Jargon Generator at ScienceGeek.Net.

How to use:

  1. Click the "Random Topic" button below to generate a randomized short answer question.
  2. Teachers: torture your students with a very random SAQ with the output or, if you like, consider turning it into a DBQ or LEQ somehow.
  3. Students: test yourself - see if you can identify examples from the CED that would help answer a question that asks what the generator spat out at you.
