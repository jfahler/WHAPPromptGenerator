AP World History Example Generator

About this tool:

This tool is designed to help teachers and students alike with creating practice questions to help develop skills and practice for the AP exam. It was designed by an AP World History teacher with the intent of creating a more dynamic and somewhat unpredictable test practice activity.

How to use:

  1. Click the "Random Topic" button below to generate a randomized topic.
  2. Fine-tune it (if you'd like) with the "Themes", "Skills", "Regions", and "Periods" buttons.
  3. Teachers: torture your students with a DBQ, LEQ, or SAQ with the output. Click here for some advice on writing AP history style prompts.
  4. Students: test yourself - see if you can identify examples from the CED that would help answer a question that asks what the generator spat out at you.
